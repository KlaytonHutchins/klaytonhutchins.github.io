## Labs 1

### Challenge: Match Phone Numbers

1. Paste this at the top of the test string:

   ```
   YES
   800 555-1234
   212 777.6789
   Phone: 800 555-6879.
   
   NO
   1234567891011
   abc def-ghij
   800 def.ghij
   Phone: 1234567891011
   ```
   Create an RE that matches the phone numbers in the text under YES but not those under NO.
